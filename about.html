How it works

01

We manage all the inventory, sourcing, sampling, and warehousing

02

You place your orders through our online ordering system. With a confirmed order, we send you the correct measurements to cut your cassette/roller and bottom bars. We pre-cut your fabrics and ship them to you.

03

You, with your minimal inventory, use a pick list generated by our system to build a parts box for a job, then you cut your other components (Tube, cassette, bottom bar…etc.)

04

When the fabric arrives, you simply Mary your parts boxes to the fabric order, and assemble them.

05

Then all you have left to do is inspect the blind, and package it.

06

Typically this process takes 10-25 minutes per shade based on what’s required (Motors, cassette, fabric inserts…etc.)

Enhanced Roller Shade Manufacturing and Sourcing

With this process you will be making roller shades using the same components and at the same quality as your
current manufacturers.

With this process you get to buy like manufacturers do, and gain some extra margin. We do the rest such as
inventory, expensive machinery, and product sourcing.

Your investment can range from $10,000 - $25,000 for roller shade assembly. Some elements can be upgraded over time (ie manual hoist upgrading to a mechanical hoist). The space needed can be as little as 500sq ft.
