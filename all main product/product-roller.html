Roller blinds are a stylish and practical window shade that can be a real focal point in the home.

This fabric collection offers a diverse range of fabrics that will transform any room, high end lustrous weaves and textures, prints that range from bold to tastefully simple and for extra privacy choose one of our effective blockouts. We even have bathrooms and kitchens covered with our moisture resistant and Ultra-Fresh* treated fabrics.Keep the streamlined aesthetic and finish off your blind with one of our cassettes and bottom bars that complement your interior and fabric choice.

For the ultimate in luxury & style, you can motorise your Roller blinds with Louvolite One Touch® rechargeable motors.
